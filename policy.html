Privacy Policy

Modern Chic cares about your privacy and safety. This policy describes what we collect about you and how we use, share, and manage this data.

Notice

By engaging with our website, purchasing any of our products, or choosing to share information with us, you agree to the collection and use of the information you provide. We will not share any of your information with a third party. We also use cookies to collect information from your product you purchase or the pages you visit. Below is a brief description of the information that we collect, how it is collected, and how we use it.

Identifiers

Indentifiers include personal information such as your name, phone number email address, and physical address. We use this data in order to identify you as a customer, market to you, personalize our items for you, or deliver to you. We obtain this data from you when you create or sign into your account or when you order an item from our site.

Personal Characteristics

Personal characteristics can include your clothing or shoe size, your gender, or your age. We use this data to personalize our items for you, market to you, analyze other data we collect, and identify other potential customers. We obtain this data from you when you create an account or order an item from our site.

Commercial Information

Commercial information includes payment information, purchasing history, and product reviews. We use this data to personalize our items for you, market to you, amalyse other data we collect, and identify other potential customers. We obtain this data from you when you browse or purchase an item from our site and when you provide us with information (i.e. write a review).

Choice

When you create an account, you have the choice of whether or not you want to be contacted again with special offers, news, and updates about the brand. If at any time you want to stop recieving emails, you have the ability to unsubscribe by clicking the button at the bottom of the email. Please know that we do not disclose any of your information to third parties.

Access

If you have any additional questions or want to review the information that we have collected, you can contact us using the information provided below. Any inaccuracies in the data will be handled between us and the customer.

Security

We care about the privacy and safety of your data. To protect your data, we use digital privacy controls by encrypting and anonymizing data where we can.
